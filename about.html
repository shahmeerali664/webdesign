Career is a very important thing in one's life. Whatever career path you choose to follow, it will impact your life greatly. Your career will define your status in a society in addition to your lifestyle. In other words, your career will determine your social circle and relationships


Career knowledge is the understanding of a career field's concepts and theories, which can come from formal or informal learning, or real-life experiences. It includes a person's perception of their career, which may include their strengths, abilities, character, feelings, and self-motivation. Career awareness is the understanding of the possibilities for long-term employment, and the knowledge needed to start and progress in a career field.
